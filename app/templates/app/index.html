How It Works

Post an RV

RV Owners Post their RV's. They must have up to date insurance, registration, and plates

The RV must be in safe, working condition

Owners have full control over when, where, and why their campers get rented

Rent an RV

The same way you rent a hotel, users can search ouRV and find their perfect rental. Communicate with the owners and negotiate terms and prices

Renters must have valid drivers license and insurance. They are responsible for damages

ouRV also caries a $1 million dollar insurance policy on all rentals

Monitor, Manage, Share

Owners: Use the ouRV platform and CarTracks to track the location and stats of the RV Renters

Renters: Use our platform to track your destinations, budget, National Park tickets, photos, and experiences. We want you to share your travels and stories so that others get inspired to explore the open road.
